# Kevin's Coaching Page | June 2026

Hey Lane! I'm managing to share an update sooner this time...! Hope you've had a good early summer so far.

I've been busy putting together a show for a local gallery that came up last minute, so lots of work framing and preparing pieces for display. It also gave me a chance to finally exhibit some of the drawings on stone. I'll share a few photos at the end. In the meantime, I have had at least a little sketching time and figured I would send you a quick update with whatever has ended up in my sketchbook.

I _think_ I've had a bit of a breakthough while watching several of the coaching clips on the Patreon where you've emphasized finding the most interesting / biggest idea and making that statement clearly and beautifully through abstract design. It's incredible how many times I've heard this advice over the years, and how many times I thought I was putting this into practice, and yet it still remains difficult to apply. Recently though it feels like something clicked and there's been a shift in what I notice in each subject. It's hard to describe and I'm not sure it's fully showing up in my work yet, but I feel a difference.

## Ink Sketching

I've taken a break from charcoal over the last couple weeks and have been doing gestures / quick sketches with ink. I enjoy the clarity a pen provides, although I often feel I don't have the patience for hatching and prefer the broader marks of charcoal or a brush for value work. I have used a pentel pocket brush pen as well, although I don't think I've quite mastered the touch it requires. I also like using two colors in the pen sketches to introduce some variety and make up for the lack of line width variation when using a very fine pen. These are all quite quick sketches, nothing too finished or developed.

I know you have an ink brush pack for Photoshop, so I think my question here is simply wondering about your experience with ink and your thoughts on ink brushes, ballpoint, washes, etc... Also, I have a new Wacom tablet in my Amazon cart at the moment since my old tablet from college no longer works with new operating systems so I'll likely be on a Photoshop brush pack shopping spree sometime soon.
